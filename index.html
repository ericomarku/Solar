Our Solar System.

Everything in this virtual model has been scaled down to 1 meter per pixel.

Then things have been scaled even further down to be viewable.

Suns scale: 1:8500

Planets and Moons scale: 1:500

Distans between Planets and the Sun: 1:610.000

Distans between Moons and their Planet: 1:17.000

Time one day per frame.
